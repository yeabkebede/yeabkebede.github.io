Yeab Kebede is an artist originally from Ethiopia and currently resides in San Jose, California. She is a multi-medium and multi-state BFA student at San Jose State University. She brings the experiences of science, color, African folk stories and patterns to her work. She questions the place of religion and culture in one’s life through her artwork. In her spare time, she reads books and researches matters related to such issues.
